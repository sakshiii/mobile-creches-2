Our Vision

A just and caring world for young children of marginalized populations to enable them to develop into competent and confident individuals.

Our Mission

To influence multiple stakeholders at National, State and Local levels by promoting Early Childhood Care and Development through providing and supporting services, enhancing capacities and campaign building.

Our Core Values

Mission more important than Organisation and Organisation more important than Individuals

Commitment to the rights of the child - in our transactions with children & our engagement with all stake holders

Accountability for performance - to all stakeholders with respect to delivery of work & use of funds

Integrity - Fair practice & respect to employees, transparency with respect to stake holders & decision making

Respect and Dignity for all stake holders - children, community, employees, donors & volunteers
