The 2nd Anita Kaul Memorial Lecture

October 17, 2018

Mobile Creches recently hosted the 2nd Anita Kaul Memorial Lecture on the Right To Education, delivered by Amita Dhanda, Professor of Law and Head, Centre of Disabilities Studies, Nalsar University of Law. The session was chaired by Aruna Roy, Founder Member of the Mazdoor Kisan Shakti Sangathan. It was a great day of learning and reflection for all.
