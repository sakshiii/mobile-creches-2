Meet The Team

MC is what MC does

And who are the people who do what MC does? It is the crèche worker who travels two hours to get to the start of a hard day’s work at a construction site centre on time; the project leader who builds rapport with local leaders in a slum basti of multiple ethnicities; the trainer who criss-crosses the country to capacitate childcare workers and parent communities; the ED who juggles people, funds, tasks and priorities; the board members who look at the long term and the big picture. We introduce a sampler, below.

Devika Singh

Co-Founder

Devika ji has a double graduate degree in English Hons from Bombay University and Leeds University, respectively. She taught English at Loretto College, Kolkatta, as a lecturer for a few years before coming to Delhi in 1969. She joined Meera, not only to co-found Mobile Creches, but to start a movement in early childhood care for the very young of the most invisible migrant community. She was responsible for the development of the day care program at construction sites, looking for entryways into the forbidding construction industry, and fleshing out the laws that applied to the industry, in how they could be tapped for the benefit of children. The program developed under her leadership and clear understanding of the complexities of the problem of migration.

She is still an active part of the advocacy team and is available in the office everyday! The MC team, today, has been nurtured by her every step of the way.

Sumitra Mishra

Executive Director

A post graduate in Special Education for persons with disabilities, Sumitra comes with over twenty years of work experience in the development sector in India. Her direct work experience with disenfranchised communities of children and women has been the pivot of the strategic programming, fund raising, organizational development and policy influence abilities. Sumitra has steered organizations through the process of program delivery and partnerships, CSR development, strategic communications and governance processes. Social justice for poor and excluded people is the fundamental agenda of her work.

Seema Sahai

Chief Operating Officer

Seema is an experienced professional with a demonstrated history of 20 years of working in the banking and financial industry. Skilled in financial management, capacity building and statutory compliances, she has pursued research in Microfinance from MDI Gurgaon on the issue of livelihood options for poor and micro enterprises. She has authored articles for the India Microfinance blog and newspapers like the Hindu Business Line. She has also presented papers on e–Governance, Microfinance, and Livelihood at various International Conferences. She has been with Mobile Creches for over two years now and leads the operations of the organization.

Neeru Bhatnagar

Senior Manager, Training & Capacity Building

A development sector specialist with 19 years of experience of working with grassroots, designing and conducting training for different levels, planning and managing projects, assessments, evaluations and resource mobilization. She has a Post Graduate Certificate in Community Resource Management and Extension from Lady Irwin College, University of Delhi. She leads the Training function at MC, and has been with MC for the last eight years.

Sonia Sharma

Senior Manager, Programmes

A Post Graduate in Social Science from Amity University with experience of 14+ years, she is heading the overall programme vertical comprising of different models of daycare programmes run and managed by Mobile Creches. Her core strength areas are programme management, proposal writing, grant management and handling resources for the programme vertical. She has previously worked in knowledge management role positions with other children-based organizations and with International organizations like Caritas India.

Chirashree Ghosh

Senior Manager, Advocacy - State Level

A post-graduate in Rural Development from Xavier Institute of Social Service, Ranchi, she has 21 years of experience in the development sector. At MC, she has gained expertise in grass root mobilization and built an understanding of state services and local government systems. She has been with MC for the last 11 years.

Sarita Sharma

Senior Manager, Accounts

Equipped with a Masters degree in Commerce, she has risen to the occasion with every widening of the MC horizon. She has gained expertise in handling grants across projects, donors and locations, alongside the financial dimensions of budgets and investments. She has been with MC for 39 years.

Shweta Bharadwaj

Manager, Human Resources & Administration
